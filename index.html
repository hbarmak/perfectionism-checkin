Adaptive and Maladaptive Perfectionism in Engineering (AMPERE)

This exploratory reflection tool translates the AMPERE framework (Adaptive and Maladaptive Perfectionism in Engineering) into an interactive browser-based experience inspired by doctoral research conducted by Dr. Haleh Brotherton.

The prototype demonstrates how psychometric research can be translated into an interactive browser-based experience for reflection and educational purposes.

This tool uses exploratory interpretation logic based on relative response tendencies rather than diagnostic or population-based classifications.
